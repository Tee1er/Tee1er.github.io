üß¨ Game of Life

A very simple Game of Life implementation, a cellular automata simulation developed by John Conway in 1970s. Life is a pretty simple simulation where cells live and die based on the numbers of surrounding cells. This is a really simple terminal-based program written in Go using minimal external dependencies. It's more of a "learning" project than an actually useful GoL implementation.
